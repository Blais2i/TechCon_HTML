About TechCon 2024

Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in a community center in Silicon Valley. What started as a modest event with just 200 attendees has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 participants annually from around the globe.

TechCon 2010 - The first conference held in a community center

Over the years, TechCon has evolved to keep pace with the rapidly changing technology landscape. From focusing primarily on software development in its early years, the conference now covers emerging technologies like artificial intelligence, blockchain, quantum computing, and sustainable tech solutions.

TechCon 2023 main stage with thousands of attendees

Each year, we've expanded our horizons, adding new tracks, workshops, and networking opportunities. TechCon 2024 represents our commitment to pushing boundaries and exploring the future of technology together.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the global technology community. We believe that technology has the power to solve humanity's greatest challenges and improve lives around the world.

Collaborative session at TechCon 2023

We are committed to:

  • Providing a platform for diverse voices in technology
  • Showcasing cutting-edge innovations and research
  • Facilitating meaningful connections between professionals
  • Promoting ethical and sustainable technology practices
  • Inspiring the next generation of technologists

TechCon 2024 continues this mission by bringing together thought leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts to shape the future of technology in a responsible and inclusive way.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has been privileged to host some of the most influential minds in technology. Here are just a few of the notable speakers who have graced our stages:

Dr. Sarah Chen

AI Research Lead at NeuroTech

Dr. Chen presented her groundbreaking work on ethical AI frameworks at TechCon 2022. Her research has influenced AI policy discussions worldwide.

Marcus Johnson

Founder of GreenCode Solutions

At TechCon 2021, Marcus shared his vision for sustainable software development and introduced tools for measuring code energy efficiency.

Priya Sharma

Cybersecurity Expert and Author

Priya's TechCon 2020 talk on quantum cryptography and future threats revolutionized how many organizations approach their security strategies.

David Kim

Lead Product Designer at VisionTech

David's 2019 workshop on human-centered design thinking transformed how many startups approach product development and user experience.

These are just a few of the incredible minds who have shared their expertise at TechCon. We're proud to have created a platform where such visionary thinkers can inspire and educate our community.
